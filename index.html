Privacy Policy for Teacher Workspace

Teacher Workspace is a Chrome extension designed to help teachers organize and manage their browser workspace.

Information Collection

The extension does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.

Permissions

The extension may use Chrome permissions such as tabs, activeTab, cookies, storage, and host permissions to provide its functionality. These permissions are only used to operate the extension features.

Data Storage

User preferences and workspace settings may be stored locally using Chrome's storage API. This data remains on the user's device.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not sell, transfer, or share personal data with third parties.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if the extension functionality changes.
